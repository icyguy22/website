Teaching kids volatile STEM Skills to prepare them for 21st Century. At B1ts N' Bytes we have a program for every stage in your kids learning journey.

Information

Our STEM based programs teach beyond what kids learn in school. As Engineers we emphasize on application and real world challenges.

STEAM(Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Math)

Our classes starts with a concept or a problem continued with solving the problem with designing and programming.

Our Vision and Mission

At B1ts N' Bytes our vision is to create the ideal learning environment in which students can thrive in.

Workshops

Our Virtual workshops gives kids the chance to learn fundamental skills of programming, Web Development, 3D designing and many more.

Design

This is where ideas become something real. We teach Design Thinking, modified as appropriate for the age of the students, to focus their thoughts on solving specific challenges.

Community Building

We want all kids, especially girls and marginalized children, to learn how to code. Thats why we offer fair and negotiable prices.

Education Mixed with Fun

We provide a fun learning experience that parents want for their children and that children want to do. Brain research shows that fun is an important ingredient for learning and memory.
